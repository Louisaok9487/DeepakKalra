About Me

Deepak Kalra

As a dedicated personal trainer at CityFitness, my mission is to empower you to become the strongest version of yourself. My journey in fitness began with a simple belief: everyone holds the potential for greatness. It's not just about lifting weights; it's about building discipline, resilience, and a champion's mindset that extends beyond the gym.

I specialize in creating bespoke training programs that focus on functional strength, hypertrophy, and sustainable results.
